About Us

Blessington Family Practice is a GP and Doctors Clinic serving the Blessington and Ballymore Eustace area. We are a well established family practice located on the Main Street, Blessington, Co. Wicklow. You can contact Dr. O’Brien and the team at their primary medical centre in the heart of Blessington town. Please click here to find a list of our available services and the members of our medical team.  If you require an appointment or online consultation please contact us at the number below.

New - Video Consultations

In response to the Covid-19 pandemic, we are now offering a video consultation option to all of our patients. This service allows you to speak face to face with one of our doctors who can provide clinical advice and guidance. Available on smartphone app and responsive website, it is secure, HSE approved and designed to give you peace of mind from the comfort of your own home. Simply call us to arrange a consultation.
