Covid-19 Vaccination Clinic - FAQ

Where is the vaccination clinic located?

Blessington Family Practice, Main Street, Blessington, County Wicklow, W91 H704
Click here to see location on Google Maps

What do I need to know about my appointment?

  • Please arrive at your appointment time. Please do not arrive early.
  • Come alone if possible. Please do not bring children, friends or other family with you unless you need assistance.
  • Your appointment will take approximately 25 minutes including observation time.
  • Do not come if you have any COVID-19 symptoms or have tested positive in the last 4 weeks. If you have any symptoms of COVID-19 infection, you should wait for 4 weeks after your symptoms started before booking a vaccine.
  • You must bring your photo ID and proof of age e.g. Driving License or Passport.

Where should I park?

  • There will be a drop off / pick up point in front of the practice for convenience.
  • There is parking available on the main street of Blessington and also a car park at the old Blessington Health Centre on the Kilbride road (220 metres from the clinic, click here to see on Google Maps).
  • Please be aware there is a 15 minute observation period after the vaccination so each appointment will take approximately 25 minutes.

Is the clinic wheelchair accessible?

Yes, but please contact the practice in advance on 045 865 527 to let us know that you require wheelchair access.

Before getting the vaccine

The vaccination clinic staff will check your details before you get your vaccine. The nurse or doctor will ask you some questions for your safety before you get a vaccine – you should answer these as accurately as possible.

After getting the vaccine

You will be asked to wait for 15 minutes after your vaccination before you can go home. Vaccination clinic staff will give you a vaccine card. You should keep this safe and bring it back at your next appointment. You will also be advised of your date and time for the second dose of your vaccine (normally 28 days after the 1st dose).

What if I need to cancel at the last minute?

Please contact the practice on 045 865 527.
